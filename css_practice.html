About Me(Silly)

My name is Ryan Moser. I was born in Johnson City, TN on April 29, 1994. I grew up there until the age of 10 when I moved to Georgia where I have lived since. I graduated high school in 2012, started my college career in 2013 and enlisted in the United State Marine Corps Reserve in 2014. I have an Associates of Applied Science in Pre-Engineering and hope to graduate with a B.S. in Computer Science in December 2021. My hobbies include hiking, backpacking, camping, live music and exercise.
